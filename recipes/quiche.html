Quiche with salmon and spinach

Description

A delicious French-style savoury tart with steamed fresh salmon and spinach. It goes well with a green leaf salad.

Ingredients

  • 25 cl liquid creme fraiche
  • 30 g grated parmesan
  • basil
  • dill
  • pepper
  • salt
  • nutmeg
  • 1 roll of puff pastry
  • 200 g frozen hacked spinach
  • 200 g salmon (fresh or frozen)
  • 3 eggs
  • 50 g emmental or gruyere (optional)
  1. Thaw spinach in the microwave or a pan
  2. Cook the salmon (steam or unsalted water)
  3. In a bowl, add the eggs, cream, parmesan, salt, pepper and a nip of nutmeg
  4. Unroll the pastry in an oven-safe dish
  5. Spread the spinach on the pastry followed by the salmon in small bits. Drizzle the herbs on top.
  6. Cover the salmon with the egg and cream preparation and sprinkle grated emmental (optional)
  7. Cook in the oven at 250 degrees Celcius for 25-30 minutes, until the quiche is nicely golden.
